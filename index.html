Welcome to UniChat!



Rules:

  1. No Profanity.
  2. No Spam/Abuse.
  3. No Emoji's.
  4. No impersonation, without explicit permission from _iPhoenix_, the site owner.

Breaking the rules may result in consequences


My goal:

  1. Provide a free, friendly, and open experience for users to chat, connect, hang out, and work.
  2. Create a bounty of anonymized, secure data that my machine-learning algorithms can learn human behavior from.

Commands:

  • /me <action>
    • Appears to everyone as: *<your username> <action>
  • /pm <user> <message>
    • Appears to <user> as: *<your username> whispers to you: <message>
    • Is not seen by anyone else.
